Our Story

Eta Pie is the brainchild of three long time friends who believe that only the limits of imagination and creativity should dictate how you create your pizzas, salads or bowls. Thus, our offerings range from the traditional to the whimsical and culinarily serious.

We encourage our valued customers to build their own masterpieces, matched perfectly to their individual preferences drawing from our palate of two pizza crusts, two bowl foundations, three blends of salad greens, five sauces, over thirty premium toppings, thirteen finishing options and six house-made salad dressings. We also invite choices from among more than twenty-five pre-curated pizza, salad and bowl options born of our chefs' inspirations.


Our Commitment

We are committed to using clean, sustainable ingredients and having everything we serve taste great. In keeping with this core philosophy, we insist upon humanely raised, antibiotic and hormone-free meat.

Our quality seafood is always sourced in accordance with the recommendations of the Monterey Bay Aquarium's Seafood Watch.

Much of our produce is certified organic and we are constantly mindful of fair trade practices and other aspects of how our suppliers operate. The bottom line is that if we wouldn't eat it ourselves or feed it to our children, you can be sure you will not find it at Eta Pie.


To Our Customers

We are, first and foremost, consumers too. Therefore, we strive to provide the same level of service and responsiveness we want to receive in our own personal interactions with other businesses. Likewise, we create our menu offerings to exacting standards and we strive to meet those standards with every order.

When we fall short (and, since nobody is perfect) we will do everything we can to make things more than right - we will solve the problem and offer something extra in recognition of the inconvenience.

With a customer first mindset, we are always looking to improve our quality and service. This means we welcome your suggestions, comments and criticisms.

*We encourage you to tell us when we succeed and when we miss the mark, so your experience is consistently the best we can provide.Your input will always be taken seriously because we recognize that serving you is the foundation upon which we succeed or fail.


Our Pledge

We believe that businesses have an obligation to invest in and engage with their local communities beyond simply serving customers. Therefore, we take our role as a member of the Ithaca and Tompkins County communities as seriously as we take our commitment to our food and unmatched customer service.

In furtherance of good corporate citizenship, we actively support community activities; we provide no-cost food to those who are food insecure; we are a second chance employer for those who, for any of a variety of reasons, face challenges in securing employment opportunities; and we cater, at no cost, birthday parties for children whose families would not otherwise be able to provide this experience for them.

We are always open to suggestions for additional ways in which we can support our community and share with our neighbors.


Join Our Team

Eta Pie is always looking for smart, motivated, hard-working, creative self-starters to join our staff. We embrace the values of acceptance, inclusivity, opportunity and membership. We hope you and our customers sense this in the atmosphere upon walking through our door.

Prior work experience in food service is not required (although we respect and value it if you have it), rather we are interested in your commitment to personal and professional growth, your dedication to doing everything you undertake to the best of your ability and your optimistic, upbeat personality and desire to ensure that our customers and your coworkers have the best possible experiences.

We offer above-industry average pay rates, a profit participation plan, paid time off, flexible scheduling and a range of other benefits and incentives to reinforce the high value we place on our team members.

The application process simple and painless. We are a second chance employer and, as such, encourage anyone who faces challenges in securing meaningful employment opportunities to apply.
